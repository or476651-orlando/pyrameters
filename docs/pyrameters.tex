Our first definition is the most importan.\\

\textbf{Definition 1}. A \textbf{graph} is a pair $(V,E)$ where $V$ is a finite set and $E \subseteq P(V)$ whose elements are of the form $\{a,b\}$, with $a,b \in V$. The elements of $V$ are called \textbf{vertices}, and the elements of $E$ are called \textbf{edges}.
